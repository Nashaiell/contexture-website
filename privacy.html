Privacy Policy

ConTexture is designed with privacy first. Your writing stays yours.

Effective date: 20 June 2026  ·  Developer: Timothy Frey  ·  rygelmir@outlook.com

Overview

ConTexture is a long-form writing app for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS. This Privacy Policy explains what information ConTexture accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected. We are committed to being transparent about our practices.

ConTexture is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your writing stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate servers that store your content, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

Information ConTexture accesses

Your writing and documents. ConTexture stores all notes, projects, character sheets, and other content you create directly on your device and in your personal iCloud Drive account via CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud container controlled entirely by your Apple ID, never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers, and accessible only by you (and anyone you explicitly choose to share with via iCloud sharing).

Calendar and Reminders. ConTexture requests access to your Calendar and Reminders to display events and tasks in the daily journal view and to create tasks from checklists in your notes. This access is used only to display and create calendar events and reminders within the app, is never transmitted outside your device or iCloud account, and is fully optional — the app functions without this permission.

Photo Library. ConTexture requests access to your Photo Library to allow you to add images to notes, character sheets, and Maps & Images folders. This access is used only when you explicitly choose to import a photo. ConTexture does not scan or index your full photo library.

Usage data. ConTexture does not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry beyond what Apple provides automatically through the App Store. We do not use any third-party analytics frameworks.

How we use your information

All information accessed by ConTexture is used solely to provide the app's features to you. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the app's stated features.

Sharing your information

ConTexture does not share your personal information with third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • iCloud sync: Your content is synced across your devices via Apple's iCloud infrastructure, governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Live collaboration: If you use ConTexture's sharing features to invite a collaborator, the specific file you share becomes accessible to that person via iCloud file sharing.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law. As we do not store your data on our servers, we have no data to disclose.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on your device (protected by your device's security features) and in iCloud (encrypted in transit and at rest per Apple's standards). We do not maintain any servers, databases, or infrastructure that stores your content. Timothy Frey (the developer) has no access to your notes, documents, or personal writing.

Children's privacy

ConTexture is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored in the user's own iCloud account and no data is transmitted to external servers, it does not present privacy risks beyond those inherent in Apple's iCloud service.

Third-party services

ConTexture does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers. The only third-party service involved is Apple's iCloud and CloudKit, which governs the storage and sync of your data.

Your rights and choices

  • Delete your data: Delete notes and projects within the app, or delete the app entirely to remove all local data.
  • iCloud data: Manage or delete your iCloud data via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud on your device.
  • Revoke permissions: Revoke Calendar, Reminders, or Photo Library access at any time via Settings > Privacy & Security.
  • Data portability: Export your notes as Markdown files using ConTexture's Export feature at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of ConTexture after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact Timothy Frey at rygelmir@outlook.com.
