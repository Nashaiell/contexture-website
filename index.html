Everything a writer needs

From first draft to finished manuscript, ConTexture keeps your writing, research, characters, and notes in one place — beautifully organised, always in sync.

✍️

Distraction-free editor

A clean, focused writing environment with customisable fonts, line spacing, and column width. Markdown supported throughout.

📁

Project organisation

Organise your work into Creative Writing, Journal, and References workspaces. Nested folders keep even sprawling projects tidy.

🧑‍🎨

Character sheets

Structured character profiles with image support, relationship links between characters, and markdown notes — all connected to your manuscript.

📅

Daily journal

A daily writing space integrated with your Calendar and Reminders. Notes, tasks, and events in one view.

🗺️

Maps & images

Store world maps, reference images, and visual inspiration alongside your writing. Insert images directly into your notes with @mentions.

☁️

iCloud sync

Everything syncs seamlessly across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac via iCloud. Your work is always where you are.

Creative Writing

Your whole world, in one app

ConTexture gives every project its own space — manuscript chapters, character sheets, research folders, maps, and reference images all live together under a single project, connected by wiki links and @mentions.

  • Wiki links between notes — type [[ to connect anything
  • @mention images directly into your writing
  • Outline view alongside your manuscript
  • Project-level word counts and page status
📖
Creative Writing
The Margin Panel

Context lives beside your words

The margin panel keeps formatting, comments, footnotes, linked documents, and images at hand without cluttering your writing space. Switch tabs without leaving the page.

  • Formatting shortcuts — bold, italic, headings, lists, code
  • Inline comments anchored to selected text
  • Footnotes panel with quick insertion
  • Linked documents and image gallery
📋
Margin Panel
Mac Catalyst

A real Mac app

ConTexture is built with Mac Catalyst — not a scaled-up iPad app. It has a proper menu bar, keyboard shortcuts for every action, sidebar navigation, and split-view editing that feels native on macOS.

  • Full Mac menu bar with File, Edit, Writing, Navigate menus
  • Keyboard shortcuts throughout
  • File → Import .md files from anywhere
  • Share & collaborate via iCloud file sharing
