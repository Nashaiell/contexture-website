Features

Everything ConTexture does, in detail.

The writing environment

A clean, distraction-free editor that gets out of your way — with everything you need always within reach.

✍️

Markdown editor

Full Markdown support including headings, bold, italic, underline, inline code, code blocks, quote blocks, bullet lists, numbered lists, and task checkboxes.

🔤

Typography controls

Choose from Palatino, New York, Georgia, System, or Monospace. Adjust font size, line spacing, and column width to suit your preference.

👁️

Live preview

Switch between edit and preview modes instantly. Preview renders full Markdown including images, headings, and formatting.

🖼️

Drawing board

Open a full drawing board directly from within your writing. Sketch a map, diagram a scene, or jot a quick idea visually — all saved alongside your notes.

🔗

Wiki links

Type [[ to link any note to any other note in your project. Navigate your world like a wiki — characters, locations, and chapters all connected.

@

@Mentions

Type @ to reference images, character sheets, or other notes inline. Images insert as Markdown and render in the preview — great for referencing maps and character portraits.

💬

Inline comments

Select any text and add a comment anchored to it. Comments appear in the margin panel alongside your writing for easy review.

#

Footnotes & citations

Insert and manage footnotes inline. Footnotes are stored as standard Markdown-compatible syntax, readable in any editor.

Project organisation

Three workspaces keep different kinds of writing cleanly separated — with a drawing board available throughout.

📖

Creative Writing

Longform fiction projects with manuscript chapters, character sheets, outline folders, maps and images, drawing boards, and research notes — all nested under a single project.

📅

Daily Journal

Daily writing space with calendar and reminders integration. See your events and tasks alongside your daily note — pull up a drawing board or insert a reminder visible across the app.

📚

References

A flat reference library for anything that doesn't belong in a project — writing notes, style guides, research clippings, and saved quotes.

🎨

Drawing Board

A full freehand drawing canvas available from within any note. Sketch maps, wireframe scenes, visualise timelines, or annotate reference images — saved directly alongside your writing.

🧑‍🎨

Character sheets

Structured character profiles with photo, biography, physical description, personality, relationships, and freeform markdown notes. Link characters to each other and reference them in your manuscript.

🗺️

Maps & images

Dedicated image folders for world maps, reference photos, and visual inspiration. Import from your photo library and reference images directly in your writing with @mentions.

Write everywhere

ConTexture works seamlessly across all your Apple devices — your writing is always where you are.

📱

iPhone

Full writing experience optimised for one-handed use. Capture ideas, write on the go, and access everything in your projects from your pocket.

📲

iPad — side by side

Use Split View to open two documents side by side — write in one, reference a character sheet, map, or outline in the other. The margin panel keeps tools always at hand.

💻

Mac — full keyboard

A native Mac experience with full menu bar, keyboard shortcuts for every action, sidebar navigation, and side-by-side split editing. Built for serious writing sessions.

☁️

iCloud sync

Everything syncs automatically across all your devices via iCloud. Start a chapter on your Mac, continue on your iPad, capture an idea on your iPhone — it's all there.

Export & sharing

Take your writing anywhere — ConTexture offers export options that most iOS writing apps simply don't.

📕

ePub export

Export your work as an ePub file — readable on any e-reader, Kindle, Apple Books, or ePub viewer. One of the few iOS writing apps to offer this directly.

📝

Plain Markdown

Export clean .md files that open perfectly in any Markdown editor. Great for moving work between apps or archiving.

📤

Rich export

Export with YAML front matter and comments preserved as footnotes — perfect for sharing with another ConTexture user who can import with full fidelity.

👥

Live collaboration

Share a note via iCloud file sharing for real-time collaboration. Both people edit the same file live — works with any Markdown editor, no ConTexture required on the other side.

📥

Import .md files

Import any .md or .markdown file from anywhere on your device or iCloud. Choose exactly which project and folder it lands in.

🖨️

Print & PDF

Print any document or export as PDF directly from the app — formatted cleanly and ready to share or archive.

Flexible pricing — no forced subscriptions

ConTexture is built on the belief that software you pay for should be yours to keep.

💳

One-time purchase

Buy ConTexture once and own it. All features available at the time of purchase are yours to keep and use indefinitely — no expiry, no locked features.

🔄

Subscription option

For users who want access to every new major release as it comes, a subscription option will also be available — entirely optional, never forced.

🆕

Major updates

Significant new versions of ConTexture will be available as a new purchase. Your existing version keeps working exactly as it did — you only upgrade when you want to.

Pricing details will be announced at launch. TestFlight beta is free.
